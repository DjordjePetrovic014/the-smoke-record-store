Born From a Passion for Music

The Smoke Record Store was imagined as a personal space for vinyl and CD lovers, built around the idea of exploring music beyond algorithms.

It's a project shaped by years of music collecting and a growing fascination with web technologies. Each feature reflects something I'd want to use myself.

More than a portfolio - this is my honest attempt at creating a digital record-digging experience.

What's the Budget Button ?

BB is a custom feature designed to help you discover the best album combinations based on your preferences - within a budget.

It uses a scoring system that balances stock, format, price, and genre - offering a smart, curated mix instead of a generic filter.

Built for collectors, diggers, and music explorers who want more for less.
